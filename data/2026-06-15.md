  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 在29个任务上使用GPT-4o-mini和GPT-4.1-mini进行50次成对和50次逐点评判重复试验，并进行温度和提示敏感性消融实验。

Conclusion: 单次LLM评判对于高风险评估往往过于嘈杂，应标准化使用多次试验聚合、位置随机化和显式不确定性报告。

Abstract: LLM-as-a-Judge is now widely used to rank model outputs, train reward models, and populate public leaderboards, but its run-to-run reliability remains under-characterized. Across judges, pairwise preferences flip on average 13.6% of the time, with 28% of questions exceeding a 20% flip rate and one question reaching 56%. These findings suggest that single-trial LLM judging is often too noisy for high-stakes evaluation, and that multi-trial aggregation, position randomization, and explicit uncertainty reporting should be standard practice. Because both judges are from a single provider, cross-provider replication remains an important next step.
