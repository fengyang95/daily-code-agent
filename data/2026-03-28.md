Motivation: 创建更接近人类智能水平的评估基准，测试AI系统在首次接触新环境时的推理和适应能力，超越传统静态测试

Method: 设计交互式推理环境，所有环境都可由人类在首次接触时解决，无需先验训练或指导，评估AI系统在相同条件下的表现

Result: 目前所有前沿AI推理模型在ARC-AGI-3上的解决率都低于1%，远未达到人类水平

Conclusion: ARC-AGI-3展示了当前AI系统在首次接触新环境时的推理能力与人类存在巨大差距，为AGI发展提供了重要评估标准

Abstract: ARC-AGI-3 is out (2 minute read) ARC-AGI-3 was designed to evaluate agentic intelligence via interactive reasoning environments. Beating it will mean an AI system matches or exceeds human-level efficiency on all environments upon seeing them for the first time. 100% of the environments are solvable by humans on first contact with no prior training or instruction. All frontier AI reasoning models currently solve under 1%.
